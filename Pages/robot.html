Rules

    Dimensions:

  • Initial: 762mm x 610mm x 1220mm.
  • During play: Expandable up to 1067mm x 762mm x 1220mm.

    Power System:

  • Must use CRCvolt batteries and approved components.
  • Maximum of 8 motors; no modifications to electrical components.

    Certifications:

  • Robots must pass safety and technical certifications.
  • Modifications require re-certification before competition.
Once the planning of the robot was done, we moved on to procuring all the components and materials required for the construction phase. This involved careful selection and sourcing of parts, as well as reusing previous projects' pieces. After gathering the necessary supplies, we began the construction process, which involved building and testing the robot, this was mainly done over the holiday break and laid the foundation for the success of our project.
